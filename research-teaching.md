  <p class="lede">
    My work combines formal demography, computational methods, and social science questions on kinship, conflict, and inequality.
  </p>

  <h2>Research Areas</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Population-level kinship dynamics and projections</li>
    <li>Family bereavement in contexts of conflict and violence</li>
    <li>Intergenerational inequality and care structures</li>
    <li>Demographic methods using surveys, genealogies, and microsimulation</li>
  </ul>
